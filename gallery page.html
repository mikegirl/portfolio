The Process & Client Brief

Plug-a-lug is a small business operating under/using the products of Earmold Australia, who already have a website. We both agreed that the existing website was too busy and confusing to navigate, with unappealing use of imagery and logos.

The clients request was to have a more simple, easy to understand menu system for his various ear/hearing protection products, as well as create a cleaner, more minimal logo for himself.

Logo & Brand Identity

The client was searching for a logo, similar to the aesthetic of my own; as he mentioned he liked both it and my business card.

We experimented with various colours and concepts before choosing the final design. The use of the ear was incorporated into the design to help identify what the business does, and also help avoid confusion as to what a "Lug" is.

The logo was eventually used on business cards, embroidered onto polo shirts and used on all the company's web platforms.
